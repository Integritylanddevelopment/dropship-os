Avatar Research Process

Finding the Underserved

Step 1: Mine Comments

Go to the top 5 competitors' TikTok/Instagram. Read 200+ comments. Look for: "but what about X?" or "I wish this worked for Y." Those are untapped avatars your competitor ignores.

Step 2: Subreddit Dive

Search Reddit for your product category + "but" or "except" or "doesn't work for." Real complaints = real avatars. Cross-reference with Meta Ad Library — are any ads targeting that sub-group? If not, you found your gap.

Step 3: TikTok Creative Center

Search your product niche. Filter by highest engagement. What hooks dominate? Who are the commenters? Reverse-engineer the avatar from the content, not the product.

Step 4: Validate with Organic

Before paid, post 5 pieces of content aimed at the new avatar. If engagement spikes (saves, shares, comments from that group) — you found a real sub-niche. Now pour paid behind the organic winner.

The DJ Remix Method

Hormozi KPI Framework

The Numbers That Actually Matter

Revenue is vanity. Margin is sanity. These are the only KPIs worth obsessing over.

Gross Margin

(Revenue − COGS) ÷ Revenue

Target: 60–70%+

If you're below 50%, the business math breaks at scale. Fix product selection or supplier pricing before scaling ads.

Contribution Margin

(Revenue − COGS − Variable Costs) ÷ Revenue

Target: 30–40%+

Variable costs = ad spend, payment processing, fulfillment apps. This is the real test of unit economics.

LTV : CAC Ratio

Lifetime Value ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost

Hormozi minimum: 3×

Below 3× you're working for your ad platform. Above 5× you have a real business. Build email/SMS to push LTV up.

KPI Formula Benchmark Action if Below
CTR (Click-Through Rate) Clicks ÷ Impressions > 1.5% (image), > 2.5% (video) Fix hook — first 3 seconds is wrong
CVR (Conversion Rate) Orders ÷ Sessions > 2.5% Fix page — offer, trust, or price anchor
AOV (Avg Order Value) Revenue ÷ Orders 3× landed cost Add bundle, upsell, or quantity break
ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) Revenue ÷ Ad Spend > 3× to break even, 5× to scale Improve creative angle or kill ad set
CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) Ad Spend ÷ Customers < 33% of AOV Tighten targeting or improve CVR
Gross Margin (Rev − COGS) ÷ Rev > 60% Renegotiate supplier or raise price
Net Margin (Rev − All Costs) ÷ Rev > 15% Cut overhead, reduce ad waste
LTV (Lifetime Value) Avg Order × Avg Frequency × Lifespan 3× CAC minimum Build email/SMS retention flows
Refund Rate Refunds ÷ Orders < 3% Fix product expectations or quality
Email/SMS Revenue % Retention Rev ÷ Total Rev > 20% Install flows: abandon cart, win-back
The Hormozi Unit Economics Test

Before scaling any product, answer: If I acquire 1000 customers at current CPA, what is my net margin after all costs? If the answer is negative, you are paying to work. Fix the unit economics before touching the ad budget.
