Gary Vee Rules — Channel Playbook
Rule 1: Cheapest First
Always max out organic (free) channels before spending a dollar on paid. TikTok and Pinterest organic before any Meta spend.
Rule 2: Volume Over Perfection
Post 3–5× per day on priority channels. Don't wait for the perfect post. Let data tell you what works, then double down.
Rule 3: Piggyback Viral
Find viral content in your niche. Copy the format, the structure, the hook. Swap in your product. Never copy content — copy the strategy.
Rule 4: Repurpose Everything
One pillar piece → 10 micro-content pieces. TikTok video → Reels → Shorts → Pinterest pin → X thread → email. One effort, 6 channels.
